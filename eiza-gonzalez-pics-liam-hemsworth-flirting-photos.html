Is She The One Who Broke Up Miley Cyrus &amp; Liam Hemsworth? Hollywood Life

Eiza Gonzalez is a Mexican actress and singer. She rose to fame with her debut role as Lola Valente in the Mexican musical telenovela ‘Lola, érase una vez.’

Other lead roles she has taken on in her career include Clara Molina on the Nickelodeon drama ‘Sueña conmigo,’ Santanico Pandemonium in the horror series ‘From Dusk till Dawn: The Series,’ Monica “Darling” Castello in the action film ‘Baby Driver,’ and Nyssiana in the film ‘Alita: Battle Angel.’

When she is not busy being a hot shot on the big screen, she is busy enjoying the company of other Hollywood hotties. Believe it or not, she made even more headlines when she got close to ‘The Hunger Games’ star Liam Hemsworth back in 2013. That year, Liam had ended his engagement with on-again/off-again partner Miley Cyrus, so it came as a complete shock when he was seen kissing Eiza one day while at her apartment in Beverly Hills.
