What is Chevy Chase's Net Worth in 2024?

Chevy Chase, an American writer, comedian, film, and television actor, has been a prominent figure in the entertainment industry for decades.

Early Life and Career

Born Cornelius Crane "Chevy" Chase on October 8, 1943, in New York, NY, Chevy Chase has built a successful career primarily in the comedy genre, marked by his physical comedy and deadpan delivery.

He gained widespread recognition as a key member of the original cast of "Saturday Night Live" (SNL) in the 1970s, where his recurring role on the "Weekend Update" segment and his catchphrase "I'm Chevy Chase, and you're not," became iconic.

Career Highlights

  • Transitioned to film, starring in successful comedies in the 1980s, including "Caddyshack" (1980), "National Lampoon's Vacation" (1983), "Fletch" (1985), and "National Lampoon's Christmas Vacation" (1989).
  • Notable role in the comedy series "Community," introducing him to a new generation of fans.
  • Received a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame, recognizing his contributions to comedy.

Stand-up Comedy
