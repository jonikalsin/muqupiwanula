Sundar Pichai | Net Worth & Income

Net Worth

The current chief executive officer of Alphabet Inc. and Google, Sundar Pichai’s net worth, is a whopping $600 million.

Since 2004, the 48 year old has been with the company in one capacity or another.

Pichai helped develop the now pervasive web browser Chrome, which pushed him up the executive ladder.

He not only got promoted, but his paycheck also got fatter as he moved uphill.

Income

His base annual salary is $2 million.

Nonetheless, in recent years he has been receiving around $100 million per year with bonuses and stock grants.

Pichai earned $100 million in the year 2015 and a staggering $199 million in 2016.

However, in 2017 he only earned $1.3 million as he was not given stock grants.

In 2018, his total earnings was a whopping $470 million.

New regulatory filings made by the company in 2020 revealed that his compensation was valued at $281 million.

The only executive with more compensation than Pichai was SpaceX’s Elon Musk that year.

Furthermore, along with stock grants, the filings also disclosed a considerable bump in the salary of Pichai from $650,000 to $2 million.

Nevertheless, the basic salary only plays a tiny role in his fortune; it’s stock grants that make him one of the highest-paid executives ever.

Alphabet also unveiled that the grants put Pichai at more than 1,000 times the median pay of its employees, which is around $258,708.

Google Stocks

Sundar Pichai was granted 353,939 restricted shares of Alphabet in April 2013 that had a five-year vesting period.

In 2013 the shares had a paper value of $184 million.

Once vested, they had a paper value of $380 million in April 2018.

The same shares had a value of $450 million when the per share trading price was at $1,300.

Sundar Pichai | Charity

Sundari Pichai has donated to several charitable organizations both personally and as a part of Google.

Sundar Pichai | Google

Sundar Pichai joined Google in 2004. Before that, he worked in management consulting at McKinsey & Company and in engineering and product management at Applied Materials.

After joining Google, he led the product management for a suite for Google’s client software products, including Chrome, Chrome OS, and Google Drive.

He also oversaw the development of other big projects such as Google Maps and Gmail.

Pichai is responsible for adding Android to the list of Google products, a project formerly managed by Andy Rubin.

He became the CEO of the company in 2015 after Larry Page stepped down from his duty.

Sundar Pichai | Career

Sundar Pichai was born in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India.

He obtained his degree in metallurgical engineering from IIT Kharagpur.

After that, he moved to the United States and received M.S from Stanford University.

He also attained an MBA from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.

As a matter of fact, Pichai was named Palmer Scholar and a Siebel Scholar at Stanford and Wharton, respectively.

He started his career as a materials engineer.

Sundar is married to Anjali Pichai and shares two children with her.

Pichai received Padma Bhusan in 2022 third-highest civilian award from the Government of India.
