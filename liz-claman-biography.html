Liz Claman Bio (Wiki)

Who is Liz Claman?

Liz Claman is an American anchor of the Fox Business Network shows Countdown to the Closing Bell, her birth name is Elizabeth Kate Claman.

Previously, she was the co-anchor of the CNBC morning television program Morning Call.

Likewise, Liz was the co-host of the program Wake Up Call as well as briefly co-anchored Market Watch, and was the anchor of the CNBC newsmagazine program Cover to Cover.

Liz Claman: Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

Liz was born in Beverly Hills, California, the United States on December 12, 1963, to parents Morris Claman and June Beverly Claman.

There is no information about her siblings. She belongs to American nationality and English ethnicity. Her birth sign is Sagittarius.

In addition, talking about her education, firstly, she attended Beverly Hills High School. Then, she attended the University of California, Berkeley.

Liz Claman: Early Professional Career

Talking about her profession, Liz Claman served as a production assistant for Paula Zahn, Jim Lampley, and Ann Curry at KCBS-TV in Los Angeles. Whereas, she did her first on-air job at WSYX-TV, KCBS-TV in Columbus, Ohio.

Similarly, she also worked as a local morning talk show host for the show, Morning Exchange on WEWS-TV. Later, she also worked as a weekend anchor at WHDH-TV in Boston.

In 1998, she also joined the CNBC network as a freelancer based in Fort Lee, New Jersey. Then, after two years, she also became a full-time journalist at CNBC.

In 2002, she also started hosting a new show Morning Call with Ted David and Martha MacCallum. Similarly, she also made several guest appearances for The News with Brian Wiliams hosted by Brian Williams.

In fact, from 2002-05, she also co-hosted a show Wake Up Call alongside Carl Quintanilla, Brad Goode, and Michelle Caruso-Cabrera. Whereas, she also worked for the network till 2007 and then moved to Fox News Channel.

Then, from 2007-14, she also hosted a weekday news show Markets Now. Likewise, since 2008, she has also been hosting a show Countdown to the Closing Bell. Eventually,  she is working as a co-anchor on Fox Business’ 4 pm show ET with David Asman.

Similarly, Liz Calman interviewed a number of business leaders on Fox including Bill Gates, Warren Buffett, Jack Lew, Alan Mulally, and Sarah Palin among others. Therefore, she published her first book, The Best Investment Advice I Ever Received in 2006.
