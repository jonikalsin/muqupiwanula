Suzanne “Stunna Girl” Brown Net Worth

Suzanne “Stunna Girl” Brown’s estimated net worth is above $600 thousand.

She rose to fame when she created some buzz on TikTok with her son Runway when she didn’t even know what TikTok was back then. Later, her younger sister in North Carolina informed her that she was going viral on the app. She grabbed attention, whether it was from her squeaky, energizing lyrical musings, her trademark “HAANNNN” slogan, or just the vibe of it all.

After someone uploaded a dancing video to her song “Runway,” which she had written and recorded back in February 2019 for the album YKWTFGO, her career on TikTok virtually overnight took off. Her distinctive vocals and musical taste are best characterized as trap music with a punch of angst and personality, highly reminiscent of Nicki Minaj’s sound.

Following the release of her song “Runway” on TikTok, the trending song became viral and millions of users made their own dancing videos for the song using the hashtag #RunwayChallenge. The drastic makeover change of ladies from ugly ducklings to absolute hotties is a recurring subject in these clips.

“More than 3 million videos have been made of people strutting their stuff to short clips of the song in what’s become the #RunwayChallenge,” according to Buzzfeed.

Talking to Office Magazine about how she got her start, she said, “I first got into rapping, honestly, when I was eight. My dad had a studio closet, and he told me to record. I was already singing for my family and stuff. But ever since that one day, I always knew how to rap. It’s kinda weird. He showed me the recording process, and how to do my ad-libs. I just never forgot it. So that one studio session stuck with me.”

Growing up she looked up to singer Sade, musically. She also listened to a lot of R&B. She told the magazine, ” I was really into old school music because that’s all my mom played. I liked Mary J Blige. It’s crazy ’cause my music is totally opposite from her, but that’s who I like.”

Not long after, Stunna signed million dollar record deal with Capitol Music Group. Before she launched her music career signed to a record label, she had a YouTube channel. Her debut YouTube video, titled “Stunna Girl-Real rap,” was made available a year before she went viral. Over four million people have listened to her song “Runway,” per her official YouTube page.

In 2021, she released an EP titled Stunna This Stunna That. Some of her latest singles are Stuntin, Sweat Freestyle, Shut Me Up, and several others.
