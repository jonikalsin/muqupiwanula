Who is Seán William McLoughlin’s‘ Jacksepticeye’?

Seán William McLoughlin, aka ‘Jacksepticeye’ is an Irish Youtuber.  Seán William McLoughlin is a professional drummer, actor, and voice-over.

Jacksepticeye is renowned for his vlogs, gaming videos, and comedic vlogs, especially the ‘Let’s Play’ series. He ranks as one of the highest subscribed channels in Ireland.

His other nicks are Jackaboy and Gaelic Gladiator. He is also a video game entrepreneur, commentator, and founder of the clothes company, CLOAK with his friend Markiplier. Seán occasionally creates online video-game sketches too.

Seán William McLoughlin -Birth Age, Family

Seán was born Seán William McLoughlin on 7 February 1990 in Ballycumber, County Offaly, Ireland.

He got his pseudonym ‘Jacksepticeye’ in Athlone, Co. West Meath, Ireland, where he grew up with his two brothers and two sisters. One of his brothers is Malcolm McLoughlin, a writer.

Jack’s pseudonym name has its origins in a childhood eye infection he had in a football accident in Athlone. A football blow injured both his eyes and in a few days, they were infected.

A child friend hollered calling him ‘Jack’s-with-the-septic-eye’ o’! and this nickname stuck. His eyes were as good as new in a few days.

Presently Jack lives in Brighton, England, United Kingdom.

In which school did Seán William McLoughlin attend?

He graduated with a degree in Hotel Management. Then as a youngster, Seán played professional drum with the heavy metal band ‘Raised to the Ground’.

Thereafter he started to create videos on video games, sketches, and occasional vlogs. He joined the tube in February 2007 and now produces and uploads two videos per day. His collaborator among others is the tuber, Markiplier, another Irishman.

Seán William McLoughlin -Rumor and Controversy

– PewDiePie

Seán co-starred as the antagonist of the second season of the YouTube Red show Scare PewDiePie which was set to premiere on 9 March 2017. However, a controversy surrounding PewDiePie prevented its release.

Later on 18 February 2017, Seán clarified the controversy with a video “Let’s Talk!” on his YouTube. He discussed that PewDiePie was cut from Maker Studios for his use of anti-Semitic imagery and language.

Seán reacted immediately by tweeting in support of PewDiePie.  However, Seán later apologized and said that he does not support PewDiePie’s verbal abuse.

Thereafter on a video the next day, he regretted that he had been ‘naïve’ to criticize the YouTuber. He reiterated that there are some unethical practices at play with the media that includes a lot of misquoting and misrepresentation’.

Revelmode network had been shut down by Disney.

-Criticism of YouTube
