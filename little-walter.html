Biography

Little Walter was born in Marksville, LA on May 1, 1930. American blues singer known for his virtuoso harmonica playing. He is best remembered for songs like “Juke” and “My Babe.” He was the featured harmonica player on recordings by artists like Bo Diddley. He left school at age 12 and began perfecting his musicianship while playing with much older blues musicians.
