Who is Chuck Liddell:

Chuck Liddell is a famous MMA Fighter. He was born on December 17, 1969 and his birthplace is Santa Barbara, CA. Chuck is also well known as, Former Ultimate Fighting Champion known as The Iceman. He compiled a career record of 21-8 and was inducted into the UFC Hall of Fame in 2009.

Chuck is originated from United States. During his career, he had high-profile rivalries with Randy Couture and Tito Ortiz.
