Uhh, poor guy, Former WWE Superstar knows how John Cena feels about his bald spot

The end of 2022 was a treat for wrestling fans over the world as they got to see their beloved John Cena in action. The Cenation leader being the “golden boy” of the company, received a warm welcome from the fans. 

John Cena and Kevin Owens defeated Sami Zayn and Roman Reigns in the main event of the edition. Cena’s participation in the fight was minimal, confined to just a few manoeuvres. The 16-time World Champion displayed incredible vigor and selflessness throughout the contest.

This was John Cena’s second appearance of the year. His first was on June 27th, when he celebrated his 20th anniversary in the company but never engaged in action. He has now fought at least once a year since his debut in 2002.

During his December 30th appearance, fans noticed a bald patch on the back of John Cena’s head. The images quickly went viral and provided material for memes. Some fans expressed sadness over the ageing of their beloved superstar.

Former WWE Superstar Paul London has now spoken about John Cena’s baldness. During a recent appearance at Café de René, London wondered whether Cena was preparing for a movie role. He said that the former WWE Champion isn’t concerned about it.

“Uhh, poor guy……He’s probably not worried about it. I would think with the millions that he has he’d be able to cover that up if he wanted to,”

Will John Cena appear at WrestleMania 39?

There has been long-running speculation that John Cena might engage in a fight at WrestleMania 39. Cena’s presence is always a plus for the company, and his appearance at the grandest stage of all would be the cherry on top. According to WrestlingNewsCo, WWE has planned Cena vs. Logan Paul for WrestleMania 39.

The Maverick has also stated his desire to face Cena at the event. During a recent edition of the Impaulsive Podcast, Logan Paul stated that it would be a birthday gift from the WWE if he faced The Cenation leader on April 1st at WrestleMania.

“Truthfully, I think me vs. John Cena would break the internet. I texted Triple H right away, ‘do you want to break the internet again?’ That’s a dream match…. let me take out John Cena. That’d be crazy.”

Logan Paul gained the respect of the wrestling community after his bout against Roman Reigns at Crown Jewel. The match is highly anticipated, and fans now have faith in Paul to put on a great performance. The Maverick is currently recovering from an MCL tear and is expected to return soon. Fans are also expecting him and Cena to be a part of the Royal Rumble Match.
