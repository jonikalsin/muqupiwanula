Biography

Tyler Seguin was born in Brampton, Canada on January 31, 1992. Canadian right-winger who won the 2011 Stanley Cup Championship with the Boston Bruins as a rookie. After the Bruins were defeated by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 2013 Stanley Cup Finals, he was traded to the Dallas Stars. He and Jamie Benn teamed up on the Dallas Stars to become one of the NHL’s highest-scoring duos. He competed in the 2009 World U-17 Hockey Challenge with the Canadian junior squad.
