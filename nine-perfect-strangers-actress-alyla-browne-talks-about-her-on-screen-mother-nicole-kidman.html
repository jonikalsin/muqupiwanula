Nine Perfect Strangers actress Alyla Browne talks about her on-screen mother Nicole Kidman

Alyla Browne has had a promising start to the world of acting as she stars as Nicole Kidman‘s daughter in the Amazon Prime series Nine Perfect Strangers. 

The 11-year-old schoolgirl spoke excitedly about filming scenes with the veteran actress for the drama series in an interview with The Morning Show on Friday.

Hosts Larry Emdur and Kylie Gillies began by asking Alyla what her favourite scene  to shoot was.  

Touch with fame: Nine Perfect Strangers actress Alyla Browne (left) talked about her on-screen mother Nicole Kidman this week and revealed what it was like working with the famous star

‘My favourite part would have to be a Lamborghini scene where me and Nicole just sat in a yellow Lamborghini for two hours talking about all sorts of different stuff and it was just amazing being with her,’ the young actress responded. 

‘And another one is we were shooting this scene and I was in this little meadow of flowers and this little butterfly came down and fluttered around me and it was just perfect,’ she added.

Larry Emdur jokingly told Alyla to make sure her parents buy her the Lamborghini from the set when she’s older. 

Best part: The young actress says her favourite scene to shoot with Nicole was ‘a Lamborghini scene where me and Nicole just sat in a yellow Lamborghini for two hours talking about all sorts of different stuff’

Alyla then talked about what it was like to have Nicole as a ‘pretend mum’.

‘She was wonderful, she’s just lovely and caring and actually, I had my eleventh birthday on-set and she gave me Pandora,’ the young actress said before showing off her bracelet to the camera. 

Alyla claims that she loves Pandora and says Nicole’s gift was the ‘best present ever she could get me’.

Present: Alyla claims that she loves Pandora and says Nicole’s gift was the ‘best present ever she could get me’ 

Browne also discussed her upcoming role as a young Jessica Watson in the Australian biopic True Spirit, which is expected to be released on Netflix in 2022.

Besides True Spirit, Alyla is also set to appear in George Miller’s next film: Three Thousand Years of Longing.

The young actress has previously starred in the Children of the Corn remake, the hit Aussie crime series Mr Inbetween and the Stan series Eden.
