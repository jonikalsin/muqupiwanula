Jeff Cavaliere - Bio, Age, Net Worth, Height, Married, Facts

Table of Biography

Jeff Cavaliere is a celebrity trainer popular as the founder of ‘ATHLEAN-X.’ Jeff Cavaliere is also a certified strength and conditioning specialist who served as the head physical therapist and assistant strength coach for the New York Mets from 2006 to 2009.

Early Life and Childhood

Jeff was born on June 28, 1975, in his birthplace, Connecticut, US. As of 2024, his age is 48 years old. He has not spoken much about his family and parents.

However, he grew up being a fan of professional wrestlers, action stars, and bodybuilders, such as Arnold Schwarzenegger.

He was always fascinated to have a shredded physique like his idols. However, it was not until high school that he started lifting weights.

During high school, he was on the school baseball and soccer team. To improve his game he wanted to get physically strong. But he was too skinny at the time and was embarrassed to join the gym.

So, he started working out on his own at home and subsequently fell in love with the idea of bodybuilding.

Education

After high school, Cavaliere enrolled in the ‘University of Connecticut’. In 1997 he graduated with a bachelor’s degree in physio-neurobiology.

Subsequently, in 2000, he completed his master’s degree in Physical Therapy. form the same university.

Career and Professional Life

As soon as Jeff graduated with a bachelor’s and a master’s degree, he kick-started his career as a physical therapist and personal trainer. But he always has his eyes on a job in the baseball industry.

His dream came true when in 2006, he was offered to work as a new physical therapist for the ‘New York Mets’.

He served the team for 3 years from 2006 to 2009 not just as a physical therapist but also as the assistant strength coach of the team.

During his time with the Mets, the team won the 2006 ‘National League East Championship.’ After handing in his registration from the baseball club, he decided to start a new chapter in his life.

He immediately authored numerous workout books and developed a training system. Subsequently, he even started his own company the ‘ATHLEAN-X Training System.’

He has a unique method of training which makes him stand out from all other trainers.

Meanwhile, he also had the chance to work with elite athletes, such as David Wright, David Beckham, Rafael Nadal, and Terrell Owens.

He soon became a world-renowned fitness guru and a social media star. Similarly, he also started a YouTube channel to share with the world his workout schedules.

The channel ATHLEAN-X which he started in 2006 now has over 10 million subscribers. He mainly posts videos related to training tips, nutrition, and other advice for a healthy body.

Relationship Status

Jeff is a married man. He got married in 2008 and is still living happily with his wife. But he is quite secretive about his personal life. This famous Physical Therapist hasn’t even revealed his wife’s name.

However, the couple gave birth to twin boys in 2016. The boys were born 13 weeks early, but thankfully they are healthy. The boys Jason and Xavier are now 4 years old.
